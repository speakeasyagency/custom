Introduction to Your POWERS Results and Personalized Success Guide 

In this assessment interpretation, you have detailed results about your workplace traits and personal well-being.  Your results are divided into seven dimensions.  Each of these broad dimensions has three related subscales.  That is 21 subscales that provide information on how your traits may impact your life in various ways!  The first several pages of this report give you a broad overview of your results. The following pages provide a guide containing detailed information to better understand and use your results. 


 THE POWERS DOES:
-Identify various strengths and areas of growth
-Indicate areas of wellness that may be out of balance
-Help you better understand yourself and can be used in conjunction with a coach or leadership program 

THE POWERS DOES NOT: 
-Provide a psychological diagnosis nor should it in any way be used as a basis for one  
-Make any indication or suggestion that you need to seek professional mental health treatment  
-Provide information intended to be used as part of a therapeutic treatment
